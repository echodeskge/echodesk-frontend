Conditions for unconditional return of goods

Everyone has the right to withdraw from a distance contract and cancel the service within 14 (fourteen) calendar days from the date of receipt of the contract, without giving any reason.

With the expiration of this period, the customer loses the right to request unconditional cancellation of the service.

HOW DO I RETURN THE SERVICE?

\* You can submit a request in multiple forms to the Echodesk.ge support team email info@echodesk.ge using the link to send a request and/or contact us via the call center at +995 510 00 33 58.

\* Echodesk.ge representatives will contact you within a maximum of 24 hours after receiving the application regarding the cancellation of services.

\* If the service is subject to unconditional cancellation, the refund of the costs will be made within a maximum of 5 business days from the date of receipt.

\* When paying by other means of payment, the user has agreed to use another means.
